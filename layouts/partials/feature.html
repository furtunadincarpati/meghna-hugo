For me, life is an exciting journey towards mastery, with change being a key part of it. Yet change has not always been easy. It required my time, commitment, compassion, and letting go. And that's on a good day. In extreme cases I noticed that the need for change can arrive as a shock, in the form of life altering events such as separation, loss of employment, poor health, etc. Since I found change to be difficult even under regular circumstances, I developed my personality and life around the ability to create smooth transitions. Over time, I realized that this ability can be useful to others as well. That is where coaching comes in. Instead of life changes being a wake up call, coaching prepares and supports you during smooth transitions.
My coaching philosophy is aimed at transforming and navigating extremes by helping you access and integrate your deepest resources.
